New card

You are about to create a new deck. Here are some tips:

  • Begin with giving your deck a name.

  • Adding cards: Add cards to your deck by dragging them to the free deck area on the right or
    just double-clicking on them. You see small version of the card appear in the deck area:

    The number at the right of the card indicates how many of them are already in your deck.
    The maximum number of one card is 2.

  • Removing cards: Remove cards by double clicking on the small preview in the deck area

  • Card counter: The small area on the bottom of the deck indicates how many cards are in your deck.
    You can have between 20 and 30 cards:

  • Save deck: When you are ready, save your deck.

Now you should be prepared to create a new deck. Have fun!
